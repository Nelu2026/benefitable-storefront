Employers invest heavily in employee benefits, yet many employees never fully understand what's available to them or how those benefits fit together.

By turning a short conversation into a personalized Benefits Map to help guide each employee's journey, supported by a Benefits Booster Kit tailored to what's most relevant to them, Benefitable helps employees better understand the benefits already available through your organization.

Help your employees better understand the benefits you already provide.

Give them a personalized Benefits Map to help guide their journey, a Benefits Booster Kit tailored to their situation, and ongoing support through a Benefits Advocate.

1. They tell us what's going on.

Employees simply describe what's happening in their lives.

2. We ask a few thoughtful questions.

A few structured follow-up questions help us understand what's most relevant to their situation.

3. They receive their Benefits Map.

Their personalized Benefits Map helps them understand how the benefits available through their employer fit together and may be relevant to their situation.

4. They receive their Benefits Booster Kit.

Their Benefits Booster Kit includes a curated collection of tools, resources, and information selected to help them better understand the benefits and resources that are most relevant to their situation.

5. They continue the conversation with a Benefits Advocate.

Conversations, backed by experienced benefits professionals designated by your organization or by Benefitable, help employees better understand their benefits and available resources.
